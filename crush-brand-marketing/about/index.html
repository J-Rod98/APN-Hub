About

Built by Someone Who's Been On Your Side of the Phone

Crush Brand Marketing exists because we ran a local service business first — quoting jobs, chasing reviews, and learning the hard way what actually makes a phone ring.

The short version

First we built it for ourselves. Then for everyone else.

Crush started inside Tuff Bros Detailing, a Central Arkansas mobile detailing company. We needed customers, the marketing quotes we got were either overpriced or vague, so we learned to do it ourselves: a website structured around real searches, a Google Business Profile that actually competes, and pages that make calling easy.

That experience is the whole point. We're not a big agency juggling a hundred accounts, and we don't talk in "brand impressions." We build the specific things that make a local phone ring, and we measure calls and quote requests — because that's what we'd want as owners.
