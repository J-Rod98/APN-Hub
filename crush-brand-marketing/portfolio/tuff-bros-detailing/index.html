Tuff Bros Detailing: A Website Built to Book Detailing Jobs

How we built a lead-generation website for a Central Arkansas mobile detailing company — the project where the Crush system was developed, refined, and proven in the real world first.

Visit the Live Site → Get This For Your Business
The situation

A mobile detailer competing in a crowded local market.

Mobile detailing in Central Arkansas is won on Google: customers search for a specific service in a specific place, compare a few options, and contact the one that looks professional and answers fast. Tuff Bros needed a website that could compete for those searches and convert the visits — not a social-media page with a phone number buried in the bio.

Full disclosure: Tuff Bros is where Crush Brand Marketing started — we built and ran this site as our own business before offering the same work to other service companies. That means every decision on this page was tested with our own money on the line, and it's why we show it as the flagship example.

What we built

Every piece of the local lead system, working together.

🏠

Conversion-focused homepage

One clear message, one clear action. Visitors immediately see what Tuff Bros does, where they work, and how to get a quote.

🧰

Dedicated service pages

Separate pages for each detailing service, so each one can match the exact search a customer types instead of competing on one crowded page.

📍

Central Arkansas location pages

Individual pages for the areas Tuff Bros serves, built with unique content — not one template with the city name swapped.

📝

Quote-qualification form

A short form that asks the right questions up front — vehicle, service, area — so every lead arrives ready to quote.

📞

Call and text actions

Click-to-call and text buttons on every page, because most detailing customers want to reach a person, not fill out paperwork.

💵

Pricing presentation

Clear packages and starting prices presented on the site, which filters out price-shoppers and builds trust before the first call.

⭐

Review integration

Real customer reviews surfaced on the pages where they influence the decision.

🔗

Local internal-linking structure

Service pages, location pages, and the homepage all link to each other the way Google expects a real local business site to.

📱

Mobile-responsive design

Built mobile-first, because that's where local service customers actually search.

🔍

Local SEO foundation

Clean titles, headings, structured data, and page structure targeting detailing searches across Central Arkansas.
