What we check

Every audit covers the six things that decide local rankings.

This isn't an automated PDF from a scanning tool. A person looks at your site, your Google Business Profile, and your competitors — and writes up what actually matters.

🔍

SEO issues

Titles, descriptions, headings, indexing problems, and technical issues quietly keeping Google from ranking your pages.

📉

Local ranking gaps

Where you show up (and don't) for your money searches — and who's taking the calls that should be yours.

🧰

Missing service pages

Services you sell that have no page — meaning no way to rank for them. Usually the biggest, fastest win we find.

📍

Missing city pages

Towns you serve that your site never mentions — invisible in Conway, Benton, and Cabot even if you work there weekly.

🗺️

Google Business Profile

Categories, services, photos, reviews, and service areas — scored against the competitors currently beating you in the Maps pack.

📞

Conversion problems

Buried phone numbers, long forms, no mobile call button — the leaks that waste the traffic you already get.
