The contractor problem

Homeowners compare three or four contractors. Google picks which ones.

A homeowner planning a fence, a roof replacement, or a concrete driveway doesn't call the first name they hear — they search, open a few sites, scan reviews, and request quotes from the ones that look established. If your web presence is a Facebook page and an unclaimed Google listing, you're not in the comparison.

And because contractor work is high-ticket, the math is forgiving: a handful of extra estimate requests a month from ranked pages can be worth tens of thousands in booked work.
