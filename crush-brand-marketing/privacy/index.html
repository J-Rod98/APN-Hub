Privacy Policy

Plain English, no legalese: what we collect, why we collect it, and how to get it deleted. Effective July 13, 2026.

Who we are

Crush Brand Marketing ("we," "us") builds websites and provides local SEO services for service businesses. This site is www.crushbrandmarketing.com. Questions about this policy go to jonathan@crushbrandmarketing.com.

What we collect

The only personal information we collect is what you type into our forms (contact form, homepage form, and website audit request). Depending on the form, that includes:

  • Your name and business name
  • Phone number and email address
  • Your website address, primary service, and service area
  • Approximate budget range and preferred contact method (audit form)
  • Whatever you write in the message field

We don't collect anything from you just for visiting the site. No account signups, no tracking pixels, no selling of browsing data.

How form submissions are handled

Forms on this site are processed by Netlify Forms, our website hosting provider. Your submission is stored in our Netlify account and emailed to jonathan@crushbrandmarketing.com. Netlify's handling of that data is covered by Netlify's privacy policy.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your message, prepare your free audit, or provide a quote
  • To contact you by phone, text, or email about your inquiry
  • Nothing else

We never sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing. Submitting a form doesn't sign you up for a newsletter or automated sequence.

Cookies and analytics

This site currently sets no cookies of its own and runs no advertising trackers. If we add a privacy-respecting analytics tool in the future, we'll update this page to say exactly what it collects.

Third-party services

Two external services appear on this site:

  • Google Fonts — loads the site's typefaces from Google's servers, which receive your IP address as part of serving the files.
  • Calendly — if you book a strategy call, scheduling happens on Calendly's site under Calendly's privacy policy.

Data retention and deletion

We keep form submissions as long as they're useful for serving you. Want your information deleted? Email jonathan@crushbrandmarketing.com and we'll remove it from our records and confirm — no questions, no hoops.

Children

This site is for business owners and isn't directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the effective date at the top. We won't quietly weaken it.
