What local SEO actually is

Showing up when someone nearby is ready to buy.

Local SEO is everything that determines whether your business appears when someone in your area searches things like "pressure washing Little Rock", "hvac repair near me", or "fence company Benton". It covers two places at once:

  • The Maps pack — the three businesses Google shows on the map at the top of local results. Driven mostly by your Google Business Profile, reviews, and proximity.
  • Organic results — the regular listings below the map. Driven by your website's structure: service pages, city pages, speed, and content that answers the search.

Most service businesses lose on both fronts for the same reason: a one-page website Google can't rank and a Google Business Profile that was claimed once and never touched. The fix isn't a trick — it's building out the pieces Google is looking for.
