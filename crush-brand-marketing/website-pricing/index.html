Read this before you buy anything

A website and ongoing SEO are two different things.

Every website we build includes an SEO foundation: clean structure, correct titles and headings, fast load times, and pages that match what people actually search. That foundation is what makes ranking possible.

But rankings aren't a one-time purchase. Staying visible takes continued work — new pages, fresh content, Google Business Profile activity, and steady reviews — because your competitors don't stop either. That's what ongoing local SEO is, and it's priced separately so you always know which service you're paying for.

Pricing questions

Frequently asked questions.

Why flat project pricing instead of hourly?+

Because you shouldn't have to guess what your website will cost. We scope the project, quote one number, and that's the number. If the scope changes mid-project, we tell you what the change costs before we build it.

Is the website price a monthly fee?+

No. The package prices are one-time project costs — you own the website when it's done. Hosting and maintenance ($99–$149 a month) and ongoing SEO (starting at $750 a month) are separate, optional services, and both are month-to-month.

What's the difference between the website and ongoing SEO?+

The website build includes an SEO foundation: clean structure, correct titles and headings, fast pages, and content that matches what people search. That foundation lets you compete. Ongoing SEO is the continued work — new pages, content updates, Google Business Profile activity, review growth — that improves rankings over time. One is construction, the other is maintenance and growth.

Can I start small and upgrade later?+

Yes, and plenty of businesses should. A landing page or five-page site now can grow into a full lead website later — we build every project on a structure that can expand without starting over.

How long does a build take?+

Landing pages usually launch in 1–2 weeks. Most websites take 3–5 weeks depending on the number of pages and how quickly we get your photos and business details. Larger authority builds are scoped individually.
