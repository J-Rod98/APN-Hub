The detailing problem

Your best work is on Instagram. Your next customer is on Google.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions.

I get all my work from Instagram. Do I really need a website?+

Instagram is great for showing work to people who already follow you — but it doesn't rank. When someone searches "ceramic coating Little Rock", Google shows websites and Business Profiles, not reels. A site plus an optimized GBP catches the customers who don't know you yet.

How do I get more ceramic coating jobs instead of $80 washes?+

With a dedicated ceramic coating page that explains the process, shows your real before/after work, answers pricing questions honestly, and makes requesting a quote effortless. Ranking one high-ticket service page changes the mix of calls you get.

I'm mobile-only with no shop. Can I still rank on Google Maps?+

Yes — Google supports service-area businesses. Your address stays hidden and your profile shows the cities you cover. Set up correctly, mobile detailers rank in the Maps pack across their whole service area.
